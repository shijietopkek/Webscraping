A scrapy spider that I built to scrape through Datacamp for details of individual courses.

DataCamp is an online learning platform for users to learn about data science through videos, quizzes, slides and exercises.
Topics covered includes R programming, Numpy, Pandas, SQL, and data science knowledge in general.
